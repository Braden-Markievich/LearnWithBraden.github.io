Classifying Sequences

Now that you understand what sequences are and how to find what they approach, you can now classify different types of sequences. First, a sequence is considered monotone if all of its terms are increasing or decreasing in unison. Next, a sequence is considered bounded if the terms lie within an upper and lower bound. Lastly, a sequence is convergent if it approaches a finite value. Examples are below.
